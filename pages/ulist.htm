Fine Art Paintings

Available Paintings

There are a number of 24"x30" oil on canvas pieces available for purchase. The "Cosmonaut" series of paintings are now finished with their gallery run and may be purchased directly. For a full list, please visit the contact page and make a request to Peter.

Paintings may be picked up from Peter's art studio in Hershey, or delivered to a location within 50 miles of Harrisburg. For locations farther away, a shipping is an option. The canvas will be removed from the frame and packaged in an insured secure mailing tube. The frame will be disassembled and shipped seperately for the buyer to reassemble at a later date.

Mural Work

Peter has several notable murals in Newark Delaware and Hershey Pennsylvania. If you would like to hire Peter to create or design a mural for your local residence or buisness please make sure to go to the contact page or send him an email at deluceart@gmail.com.

Murals are a significantly time consumming project to undertake and as such may require a more thorough pitch to commission. Peter has notable expertise creating large wall sized oil paintings, video projection wall murals, and large scale wall print digital paintings. Please make sure to include as much detail as possible in your message.
