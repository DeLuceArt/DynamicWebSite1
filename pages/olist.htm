Fine Art Prints

Own a DeLuce Art Print

The majority of Peter DeLuce's digital paintings are available to purchase as prints on Etsy. Each 8"x10" high quality luster print is hand signed with a matte frame. Additionally, signed 24"36" movie poster are available for several popular paintings as well.

Peter's art and illustrations may also be found with liscenced online vendors. One of these, the SpaceX Fanstore, is a close buisness partner with Peter. He is one of their featured artists and will be featured on their popular social media sites. Occasionally, discount codes can be found on their instagram wich can be used to get a great deal on some DeLuce Art merchandise.

Print on Demand Options

Some of the best ways to share art is actually not on a canvas or within a frame. Thanks to the internet and the rise of eCommerce, print on demand breaths new life into creative expression. Now you can buy Peter's illustration work on phone cases, shirts, and coffee mugs.

Sites like Redbubble and Society6, allow Peter the freedom to create and design a new kind of brand. His store, DeLuce Art, is starting to take off thanks to how customizable the printing options are. With print on demand, Peter designs the art and a production company handles shipping and printing. This lets him focus on what he is good at, making art.
