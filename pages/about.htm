About the Artist

Peter D. DeLuce



Peter DeLuce is a digital and traditional painter from Hershey PA, and has been working professionally as a artist since 2015. He studied Fine Arts at the University of Delaware and received his Bachelors from there in 2017. Much of his daily work involves licensing his illustrations and designs to online distributers. Peter frequently attends community art festivals across the east coast where he sell prints, paintings, and other merchandise depicting his cosmic themed art. He is also the owner of DeLuce Art, which is the name for his online business and print on demand stores.

After becoming widely recognized online with his viral paintings in 2018 Peter has since received notable recognition from the scientific community for his work. From Elon Musk to Scott Kelly, his art has managed to find praise from even the most high profile scientists, astronauts and engineers. The success of his social media presence relates directly to his passion for space and science.

Currently, Peter is obtaining an Associates in Web Development from HACC. He plans to fully utilize the skills he obtains from studying web development to further enhance his digital media business and ability as a digital artist.
