Collaborations

If you are interested in collaborating with Peter DeLuce or DeLuce Art, please fill out the contact form on this page. Leave your name, email,  desired mediums, and a brief message. You will typically recieve a response back within 3-5 buisnessdays.

The demographic information is optional to input and is primarily there for research purposes. If you would like to have a more detailed conversation about a commission, you can try reaching out to Peter on social media. His inbox can fill up quickly on days he posts his artwork though so to guarentee he sees your message, this is the best place to contact him.

Commission Work

Peter does not typically take commission requests, but for the time being he is open to doing them. Generally his work centers around space, science, or athletics. Should your commission be something starkly different, he may not accept.

Make sure to include as much detail as you can and include your prefered price range. Peter typically charges by the hour for a project, but the final price is also determined by the cost of materials and the urgency of the project's completion date. Shipping addresses will also be factored in depending on the medium requested.
