Peter DeLuce Art Gallery

Welcome to DeLuce Art


Hello to all those space art enthusiasts out there. This is the temporary online gallery and general information site for artist Peter DeLuce. Over the next few months expect to see this site change a little as it gets gradually fixed up.

Peter DeLuce has been sharing his work online for the last 2 years on Instagram and Facebook, but now has a web page that he can call his own. On this site you will find plenty of great art and alot of information about Peter that you might not have known before. Get ready to find a new artistic take on the cosmos!

Paintings and Prints


Though Peter is capable of working with several different art mediums, his paintings are the most recognizable. If you are interested in owning a piece of the interstellar skys he paints, check out the orders section in the top menu bar.All of his digital paintings can be bought online now.

You can buy dozens of high quality art prints through Etsy or on the DeLuce Art Redbubble page. If you are more interested in the physical artwork, there are some available gallery paintings available for purchase.

Contact or Commission


If you are interested in reaching Peter please check out the contact section of the website. He is also open to taking commission work at this time, so if there is a special person in your life that would look good with a cosmic portrait, now is your chance to get one!

For any licensing information, please send an email directly to deluceart@gmail.com. All other collaborations or contract work may be communicated through the contact form of this website. Please allow up to 3-5 business days for his response.
